Teaching

I enjoy teaching mathematics at both the undergraduate and graduate levels. My teaching interests include algebraic topology, geometry, algebra, and analysis. I believe that effective teaching combines mathematical rigor with intuition and encourages students to develop independent problem-solving skills.

Teaching Philosophy

My approach to teaching emphasizes conceptual understanding together with mathematical rigor. I encourage students to actively engage with proofs, examples, and problem-solving so that they develop both technical proficiency and mathematical intuition.
