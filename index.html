About Me

I am currently an NBHM Postdoctoral Fellow in the Department of Mathematics at Indian Statistical Institute, Kolkata , where I am mentored by Prof. Samik Basu .

Prior to joining ISI Kolkata, I held a postdoctoral position at the Tata Institute of Fundamental Research (TIFR), Mumbai , where I worked with Prof. Radhika Gupta .

I obtained my Ph.D. in Mathematics from the Indian Institute of Technology Bombay under the supervision of Prof. Rekha Santhanam .
